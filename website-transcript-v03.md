# Open questions for v0.3 review

1. **Block 12 — the family tableau.** *"In a kitchen. A child, watching."* — I want this to land as a Norlha-style image, not a stock photo description. Alternatives if it feels wrong: *"One pair of hands. Another, watching."* (universal, less domestic) · *"Practised at home, before anywhere else."* (says it instead of showing it) · *"A father, in a kitchen. A child, watching."* (specific, more "father" weight — closer to brief p.82 framing).

2. **Block 14 — the time block voice.** *"Three thousand years old. Thinking about the next thirty."* — I dropped the "we are" to keep it tight. Compare: *"We are three thousand years old. We are thinking about the next thirty."* (warmer, more explicit) · or even *"Three thousand years behind. Thirty in front of you."* (puts the reader's life inside the frame).

3. **Block 20 — the triplet placement.** Right now: name → triplet → tagline. Could also be distributed across the page (Philosophy at end of Body, Ritual at end of Practice, Ceremony at end of Family) so each section names itself. Stronger architecturally, but adds 3 blocks and risks feeling academic. Worth a test.

4. **Block 11 — "And then you carry on."** Kept because you approved it. But it's the closest the page gets to playful. If the page wants pure oracle, this is the line to cut.

5. **Block 08 — "moments of care".** Your note said *"maybe personal moments of care smth like that?"* — I picked the shortest phrasing. Alternatives: *"in care, repeated"* · *"in the time you give yourself"* · *"in small acts of attention"*.
